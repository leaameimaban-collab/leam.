Our Rich History

Rosales National High School stands as a beacon of learning in the heart of Pangasinan. Established with the goal of providing accessible, high-quality secondary education, the institution has evolved into a premier center of excellence.

Through decades of dedication, we have produced leaders, professionals, and skilled individuals who contribute significantly to our nation. Our commitment to "Quality and Excellence" remains the cornerstone of our operations.
